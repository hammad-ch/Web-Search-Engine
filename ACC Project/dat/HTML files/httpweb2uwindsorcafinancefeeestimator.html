Tuition Fee Estimator


The fees shown are the approved fees for 2021-2022 academic year. Fees may change for the Summer 2022 term and should only be used as an estimate for the fees in the future. The fees calculated are per term and will be based upon the information you provide below. To obtain the exact fees you owe for the current term, please goto UWinsite Student.

If you are in a joint program with a partner institution, you will need to contact the other institution for their fees and payment information, if applicable.
