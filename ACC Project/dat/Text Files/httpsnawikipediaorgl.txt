Ada wei animwen emenengame Wikipediya ian Dorerin Naoero ebakin 1,667 peij. Omo peij: Ngea Naoero, bet Ripubrikin Naoero (Ing.: Nauru, Republic of Nauru), eiy ngea eben Otsianiya. Ina won ata ma aeoq itubwang: Aiwo, Anabar, Anetan, Anibare, Baiti, Boe, Buada, Denigomodu, Ewa, Ijuw, Meneng, Nibok, Uaboe me Yaren. Pretiden: Lionel Rouwen Aingimea.
